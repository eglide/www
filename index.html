Welcome to Eglide.

Durant les travaux, l'exposition continue.

We're baking a great shell hosting service. Claim a little space for your files, an IRC connection for you or your bot, a website. Something where to code, experiment, connect with like-minded people..

Something different. The project is fully managed as configuration-as-code with a Salt repository. That means you can contribute to the servers configuration, like you contribute to any other open source project.

Current status

We're currently in closed beta. Former Grip users can request an account.
